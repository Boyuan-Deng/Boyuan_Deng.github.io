I love using my design skills to make the world better for everyone.

Ever since I was a kid, I wanted to help people. I was born with a cleft palate, so being faced with challenges from an early age, I found joy in helping others overcome theirs. I still remember my elementary school friends coming to me for advice regarding their "love" life... heh.

Years later, I managed to get into the most prestigious higher-ed school in Serbia (my home country), to study psychology. I dreamed of becoming a therapist, but somewhere along the way, I discovered I preferred the more logical, data-driven approach in the field of cognitive assessment. Being an overachiever, I undertook the most difficult Master's Degree project I could find, and ended up helping to improve the state of cognitive assessment in Serbia.

Around this time, I started getting serious about design. I had made a few websites for friends (in tables!), and realized this was something I was actually good at. I moved half way across the world, to the U.S., and co-founded a design agency with my amazing wife.

Along the way, I learned to incorporate my empathy, passion, and skills I learned in psychology into my design work. I helped my clients identify and solve their core problems, and co-created solutions that improved their business, and the lives of their users.

Today, as I move away from remote contracting, I'm looking to find a company that is passionate about design  and making the world a better place for everyone.
